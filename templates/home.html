  • Our Journey and Expertise

    Inception and Legacy: Sunelec was founded in 1984 by Sunil Vora and the late Pradip Shah, and our journey continues to thrive today. Over 35 years of dedicated service have solidified our position as a leading Electrical and Solar Contractor and Consultant in the industry. Electrifying Success: With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we have successfully completed over 1000 HT+ Electrical projects, including HV and EHV substations up to 132 Kv, and more than 500 MW of Solar Power plant projects. Our expertise spans the entire spectrum from project concept to commissioning. Liaison and Collaboration: Our team boasts strong liaising expertise with various Government bodies, ensuring smooth processes for permissions and approvals. We pride ourselves on maintaining cordial relations with consultants and clients, fostering an environment of collaboration and efficiency.

    Empowering Industries with Solar Energy

    Solar Revolution: In 2011, Sunelec embarked on a remarkable journey with the establishment of our Solar division. We harnessed the limitless potential of solar energy, leveraging the latest PV technology to make a significant impact. Affordable Solar Energy: At Sunelec, our vision is clear - to bring affordable solar energy to all, especially industries burdened by expensive state power utilities and discoms. By doing so, we help businesses save substantially on electricity bills, ultimately enhancing their profitability. Beyond Power Supply: Our strengths lie in our highly experienced team of Engineers, efficient planning, technical expertise, prompt service, timely project completion, sound financial stability, and the ability to work tirelessly to deliver cost-effective solutions. Beyond power supply, we advise and consult with industries, enhancing their electrical distribution networks and systems with cutting-edge technology. Expanding Presence: With offices strategically located in Mumbai, Palghar, Silvassa, Ankleshwar, Vadodara, and New Delhi, Sunelec is committed to providing the finest electrical and solar solutions and services to our valued clients and industries.
